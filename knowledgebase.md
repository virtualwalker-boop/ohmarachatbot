# TECHNICAL REPAIR DOMAIN 1: LAUNDRY SHOP EQUIPMENT & WASHING MACHINES
- EQUIPMENT COVERAGE: Commercial laundry shop setups, residential washing machines, including top-load washers, front-load washers, and twin tub units.
- TECHNICAL SCOPE: Diagnosing and fixing mechanical, electrical, and electronic system failures.
- COMMON SYMPTOMS & SOLUTIONS:
  * SYMPTOM: Washing machine or commercial washer is leaking water.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Inspection and replacement of door bellows gaskets, faulty water inlet valves, damaged internal tubs, or deteriorated drain hoses.
  * SYMPTOM: Washer fails to spin, or drum is not turning.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Diagnostic evaluation and replacement of worn-out drive belts, faulty drive motors, broken lid switches, damaged door locks, or failed mechanical gearboxes/transmissions.
  * SYMPTOM: Water fails to drain out of the tub or drum during or after a cycle.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Unclogging or replacing blocked drain pumps, clearing debris from internal drain filters, or repairing electrical wiring faults to the pump assembly.
  * SYMPTOM: Excessive vibration or loud banging noises during the high-speed spin cycle.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Inspection and replacement of broken suspension rods, worn shock absorbers, or unbalanced load sensor microswitches.

# TECHNICAL REPAIR DOMAIN 2: REFRIGERATION & AIR CONDITIONED SYSTEMS
- EQUIPMENT COVERAGE: Household refrigerators, commercial chillers, window-type air conditioners, and split-type air conditioner units.
- COMMON SYMPTOMS & SOLUTIONS:
  * SYMPTOM: Refrigerator or commercial cooler is not cooling or keeping food frozen.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Defrost system diagnostics, thermostat replacement, start relay replacement, evaporator fan motor replacement, or advanced compressor unit repairs.
  * SYMPTOM: Air conditioner is blowing warm air or has weak airflow.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Periodic deep cleaning and general maintenance, fixing electrical failures in control boards, replacing faulty run capacitors, or resolving air restrictions.

# TECHNICAL REPAIR DOMAIN 3: KITCHEN APPLIANCES & HEAVY DUTY EQUIPMENT
- EQUIPMENT COVERAGE: Gas ranges, built-in ovens, commercial and household cooking stoves, and heavy-duty kitchen electronics.
- COMMON SYMPTOMS & SOLUTIONS:
  * SYMPTOM: Gas range burners or oven elements failing to ignite or heat.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Testing and cleaning clogged gas burner orifices, replacing faulty spark ignition modules, repairing broken safety valves, or swapping failed electrical heating elements.

# TECHNICAL REPAIR DOMAIN 4: AIR MOVING & SMALL HOUSEHOLD ELECTRONICS
- EQUIPMENT COVERAGE: Electric fans (industrial and stand fans), microwave ovens, rice cookers, blenders, and essential everyday household electronics.
- COMMON SYMPTOMS & SOLUTIONS:
  * SYMPTOM: Electric fan blades move very slowly, hum loudly, or do not spin at all.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Electric motor rewinding, replacement of degraded motor run capacitors, cleaning/lubrication of seized shaft bushings, or total motor assembly rebuilding.
  * SYMPTOM: Small kitchen appliances (Microwaves, Rice Cookers) fail to turn on or heat.
    - REPAIR SCOPE: Identifying blown thermal fuses, replacing faulty magnetrons, repairing control door switches, or replacing defective main PCBs.
